- Be conversational
- Read out loud as you write
- Vary your pacing
- Let yourself feel emotion
- It’s okay to breathe
- It’s okay to leave silence
- Use conversational tone, not delivering a speech, but telling a story to someone
- Only your voice is carrying this, smooth, read in one take
- Popped P’s
- Distance from mic- at least 6 inches from front, or closer to the side
